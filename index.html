About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University, where I am being advised by Christo Wilson. I completed my undergradute in Computer Science from School of Science and Engineering, LUMS, Pakistan.

My current research focuses on web security & privacy. In particular, I am studying the online advertising ecosystem on the web to understand how it impacts users' privacy. My goal is to make the online advertising ecosystem more transparent to enable users make informed decisions regarding their personal information. I have previously worked on projects aimed at limiting abuse on online services such as Facebook and Twitter.
